Why it exists

Microsoft Photos got worse.
We got tired of it.

Windows used to have a great photo viewer. Then it became an ad for OneDrive and Clipchamp. Aura Gallery is the alternative — inspired by Photos Legacy, built for people who just want to look at their photos.

  • 🗂️You control your folders. Nothing is auto-loaded from anywhere.
  • ☁️No cloud required. Your photos stay on your PC.
  • 🔓Open source under the MIT license. Free forever.
  • 🧩Part of the Aura suite — a family of clean Windows apps.
